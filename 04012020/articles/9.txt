The novel coronavirus has brought distance learning to schools across the world with much controversy. Many question the efficiency of such classes - how will teachers ensure the attention of students during lectures and discussions? Others question the morality of the learning style - how will underfunded schools prepare for online classes without the tools to do so? Well, all these criticisms of distance learning are both ridiculous and unfounded. Who cares about any of those problems when distance learning solves infinitesimally more issues? From remedying climate change to promoting mental health and fostering emotional awareness and romantic confidence, distance learning provides extensive benefits that outweigh any harms.
